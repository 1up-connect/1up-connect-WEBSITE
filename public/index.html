Who We Are

Built for Melbourne's Youth

1UP Connect is a West Melbourne youth community initiative creating spaces where young people can compete, connect, and grow. We run events that go beyond entertainment — from padel and futsal tournaments to mental health workshops and school programs.

We partner with local venues, community organisations, and government initiatives to keep our events accessible, affordable, and real.
